How it works

  • Prosopo system is a low-trust human verification system allowing Distributed Apps (Dapps) in the Polkadot ecosystem to detect and block network activity generated by automated bots. This is achieved without reliance on centralised third parties.
  • A network of Providers supply Dapps with verification methods, such as CAPTCHAs, that identify if their users are human or not.
  • Providers that supply CAPTCHAs can benefit from having their data labelled by Dapp Users, rewarding Dapps in the process.
  • Providers may also offer alternative verification methods and charge Dapps a fee for the service.
